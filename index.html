About Me

My name is Greg Pfeifer and I am an aspiring web developer! I graduated from Rutgers University in 2018 with a B.A. in Economics and began working at a mortgage company as a loan underwriter. After five years in this industry, I decided I want to pursue a career in information technology, a subject that has always fascinated me. My goal is to become proficient with all the tools available to a web developer and pursue an entry-level position upon completion of this course.

In my free time, I like to work out and play drums and guitar. I also love to read; Stephen King is perhaps one of my favorite authors!
